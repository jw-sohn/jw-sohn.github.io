Jiwoong Sohn

PhD Candidate, ETH Zürich

I'm fortunate to be advised by Michael Moor (Medical AI Lab) and Torsten Hoefler (SPCL). I work on large language models and agentic systems for knowledge-intensive reasoning: post-training, process reward modeling, personalization, retrieval-augmented generation, and test-time strategies. Previously at Yale and Korea University.
